These traditional cookies are widely known for their soft and chewy centre, baked with buttery-rich dough and chocolate chunks, Chocolate chip cookies is not just any cookies but culture.It is the most common cookies of all time for events like: birthday parties, picnics, get-togethers etc. Let's dive into the recipe, nows!

The Basic Recipe

Below is a classic recipe for our chewy chocolate cookies recipe that makes a rich chocolate flavour

Ingredients:

  • One (280g) cup of softened butter
  • Two (2) cups (400g) of white sugar
  • Two (2) large eggs
  • Two (2) cups (250g) of all-purpose flour
  • Two (2) teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • One-eighth (1/8) teaspoon of salt
  • Two (2) cups (340g) of semisweet chocolate chips. This is optional

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar by mixing the softened butter and the white sugar. Mix until creamy, smooth and fluffy
  2. Add the eggs one at a time and the vanilla extract the fluffy mix. Mix well till uniform
  3. In another bowl, mix the flour, baking soda and salt. Mix properly and add the dry mixture to the previous wet mixture and mix well till uniform
  4. Fold in the semisweet chocolate chips with a spoon. Optional :Chill the dough in a refridgerator for about 30 minutes to obtain thicker cookies for essential taste
  5. Preheat the oven to 175 Celcius (350 Fahrenheit)
  6. Lay parchment paper on the baking tray and scoop the cookie dough of preferable size onto the baking tray
  7. Bake for about 10 - 12 minutes until golden brown at the edges
  8. Let them cool for about 5 minutes on the baking tray amd then you can move onto a cooling rack
