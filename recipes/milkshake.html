Description

A classic delicious milkshake is a creamy blend of various ingredients of choice, but mainly made from ice cream and milk. I can't be the only one who caught a whiff of that? The key advantage of milkshake is the recipe. Its recipe can be manipulated to our taste of choice from various toppings through to countless mix-ins, giving a rainbow of flavours in one. Mmm, love the sound of that? Let's jump right into the recipe, yum !


The Basic Recipe

Below is the recipe that makes one serving but can be easily doubled, tripled and so on. Remember, we're responding to our taste buds

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 large scoops of your favourite ice-cream. The most popular flavours are usually: vanilla malt, chocolate, strawberry and oreo-cookies cream but any flavour works fine
  • A quarter (1/4) or half (1/2) cup of chill whole-cream milk . Start with less milk and add more till your desired consistency
  • Optional: Toppings, flavourings (vanilla extract), mix-ins and garnishes (sprinkles, whipped cream, caramel sauce etc.)

Steps:

  1. Chill the serving glass . This is not necessary but for better results, chill the serving glass in the freezer while you prepare the milkshake
  2. Soften the ice-cream. For easy blending, the ice-cream must defrost or soften at room temperature for about 10-15 minutes
  3. Add ingredients to a blender in the following order: For blender blades to work efficiently, we add liquid first (whole-cream milk in less amounts first) then flavouring (vanilla extract if using any) and finally, the softened ice-cream
  4. Serve immediately into your chill serving glass. Add your preferable toppings or garnishes to suite taste
  5. Enjoy your milkshake!
